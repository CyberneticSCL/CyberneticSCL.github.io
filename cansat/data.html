Data from previous pre-flight test runs were fit to a linearalized calibration curve based on the relationship between the number of beeps emitted by the 555 circuits and the ambient temperature and light level. The data we collected from our CanSat during the flight demonstration was compared to this calibration curve to determine the ambient light level and satellite thermal conditions during the flight.This data can be seen in the above tables along with the extrapolated light and temperature levels.
